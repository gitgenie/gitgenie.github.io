Network Planning & Optimization

Network Planning for Mobile Network Operators in India and APAC is one of the forte of NIW. NPO as a process involves the following modular activities.

RF Survey
  • Site visit to analyze and authenticate site conditions. Gather information- GPS co-ordinates, geographical terrain details etc.
  • RF Planning and predictive software tools to be used to generate RF survey reports
    • Assimilate the gathered information to determine the number and placement of access points that allows optimal coverage throughout the facility.
    • Suggest the best and 2 additional Site locations for the best yield in coverage, power, angle and convenience.
Drive testing

Drive Test is conducted to evaluate the RF coverage of a particular Cell and to ensure that there are minimal blind spot for a mobile network operator in terms of coverage, Handover,RF parameters etc, . The coverage data collected by drive test tool as Log files is analyzed to evaluate various RF parameters of the network.

Once the Drive test is complete, the NW optimization parameters are captured from the logs to be input to the MNO.
